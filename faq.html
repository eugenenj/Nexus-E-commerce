Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about orders, payments, delivery, and after-sales support.

What are your shipping costs?

Standard shipping is free on orders over $75. Otherwise, a flat $6.99 fee applies.

How long does delivery take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery takes 1–2 business days.

What is your return policy?

You can return unopened items within 30 days for a full refund.

Do products include a warranty?

Most Nexus products include a 1-year limited warranty; select devices include 2 years.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
